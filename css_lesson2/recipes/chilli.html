Chilli con Carne

DESCRIPTIVE NICETIES

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 heaped tsp hot chilli powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 500g lean minced beef
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • ½ tsp dried marjoram
  • Thumbnail-sized piece of dark chocolate
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 410g can red kidney beans
  • Plain boiled long-grain rice

Recipe

  1. Prepare vegetables. Dice onion, about 0.5cm square.
  2. Cut 1 red pepper in half lengthways, remove stalk and wash the seeds away, then chop. Peel and finely chop two garlic cloves.
  3. Start cooking. Put your pan on the hob over a medium heat. Add 1tbsp oil and leave for 1-2min until hot.
  4. Add the onion and cook, stirring fairly frequently, for about 5 min, until the onion is soft, squidgy and slightly translucent.
  5. Tip in the garlic, red pepper, 1 heaped tsp hot chilli powder, 1 tsp paprika and 1 tsp ground cumin.
  6. Give it a good stir, then leave it to cook for another 5 min, stirring occasionally.
  7. Brown 500g mince. Turn up the heat on the veg pan, and add the mince, breaking it up as you do so.
  8. Keep stirring and prodding for at least 5 min until the mince is uniformly spread and there are no pink bits. Make sure you keep the heat hot enough for the meat to fry and brown, rather than simply stewing.
  9. Prepare the stock and add to the pan.
  10. Add tomatoes, marjoram, (sugar if not using chocolate,) salt and pepper. Squirt in about 2 tbsp pureé and stir the sauce well.
  11. Simmer gently. Bring the whole thing to the boil, give it a good stir and cover the pan. Leave gently bubbling for 20 mins.
  12. Check the pan occasionally to stir and make sure the saue isn't boiling dry. If so, add a couple tbsp water and make sure the heat is low enough.
  13. Drain and rinse kidney beans and stir into chilli pot, along with chocolate. Bring to boil again, and simmer for 10 min, adding water if it looks dry.
  14. Taste and season. Will probably require more seasoning than expected.
  15. Once ready, leave to stand for 10 min to allow flavours to mingle.
  16. Server on a bed of rice with sour cream on top. Alternately, pile it on tortilla chips and sprinkle with grated cheddar. Or, wrap in a tortilla with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes and guacamole for a burrito.
